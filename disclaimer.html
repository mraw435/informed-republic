Department of Defense Name

Informed Republic refers to the Department of Defense by its statutory name as established by the National Security Act of 1947, as amended. A 2025 executive order directed use of the name "Department of War" in some contexts; however, this rename has not been enacted into law by Congress as of the date of this disclaimer. Where relevant, we note both names and the status of any pending legislation.
