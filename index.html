About

Built for an informed citizenry

Informed Republic was created to provide a comprehensive, nonpartisan resource for understanding the United States federal government. We believe every American deserves access to clear, accurate information about how their government works and what it is doing.

Our goal is simple: help every reader understand (1) how government operates, (2) who their elected officials are, and (3) what's happening in Washington — every single day.
